Learning based on projects should be easier and more interesting, but the gap goes a step further.
Rading is not a learning method, but more help that provides support to individuals who learn something new, such as the use of a new computer program or start a new project.
The stages can be both virtual and true, in other words, the teacher is a shape of the stage, but this also applies to a small clip in Microsoft Office.
The virtual stages are the internal part of the software and are supposed to ask questions, encourage and interpret procedures that could be an excessive challenge for the student.
