Ancient China had a unique way of distinguishing different time periods;Every time of China or every ruling family was a separate dynasty.
There was also an unstable period of divided provinces among each dynasty.The most famous of them was the period of the three kingdoms, which lasted 60 years between Han and Jin dynasties.
During these periods, a fierce war took place among many nobles fighting for the throne.
The epoch of the three kingdoms was one of the bloodiest epochs in the history of ancient China, when thousands of people died in the fight to get the throne in the Great Palace in Si-An.
