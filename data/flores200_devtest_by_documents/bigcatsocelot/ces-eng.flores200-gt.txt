The steel likes to eat small animals.If they have the option, they catch monkeys, snakes, rodents and birds.Almost all the animals that hunt steel are much smaller than himself.
Scientists believe that the steel is monitored and found animals to eat (prey) according to the smell, when they sniff, where they moved on the ground.
At night they see very well thanks to night vision and can move very quietly.The steels hunt by merge with their surroundings and then jump on their prey.
