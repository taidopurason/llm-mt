The Paralympic Games will take place between 24 August and 5 September 2021. Some events took place in other locations throughout Japan.
Tokyo will be the only Asian city to host the Summer Olympics twice, the first being held there in 1964.
If you have booked flights and accommodation for 2020 even before postponing, you may be in a difficult situation.
Cancellation policies vary, but most coronavirus cancellation policies of late March don't apply to July 2020, when the Olympic Games are scheduled to take place.
Most tickets are expected to cost between 2500 yen and 130,000 yen, and standard tickets will cost about 7,000 yen.
