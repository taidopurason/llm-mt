Deputy Prime Minister Julia Gillard claimed during the 2010 federal election campaign that she believed Australia should become a republic at the end of Queen Elizabeth II's reign.
34 percent of those polled agree, saying they want Queen Elizabeth II to remain Australia's last monarch.
At the extremes of the results, 29% of those polled think Australia should become a republic at the earliest opportunity, while 31% believe Australia should never become a republic.
