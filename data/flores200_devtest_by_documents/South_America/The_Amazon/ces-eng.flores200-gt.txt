Amazon is the largest and the second longest river on the ground.It flows more than eight times more water than the second largest river.
The Amazon is also the widest river on the ground, in some places it is up to six miles wide.
The whole 20 percent of the water flowing from the rivers of our planet into the oceans comes from the Amazon.
The main flow of the Amazon is 6 387 km (3,980 miles).It collects water from thousands of smaller rivers.
