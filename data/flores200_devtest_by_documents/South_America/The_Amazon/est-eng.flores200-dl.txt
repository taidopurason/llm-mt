The Amazon River is the world's second longest and the largest river in the world. It carries eight times as much water as the world's second largest river.
The Amazon is also the widest river on Earth, at six miles wide in places.
20% of the water that flows from the world's rivers into the oceans comes from the Amazon.
The main Amazon river is 6387 km (3980 miles) long. It receives water from thousands of smaller rivers.
