The Amazon River is the second longest and largest river on Earth. It contains more than 8 times as much water as the second largest river.
The Amazon, whose regions are up to six miles wide, is also the widest river on Earth.
As much as 20 percent of the water flowing from the planet's rivers into the oceans comes from the Amazon.
The main Amazon River is 6387 km (3980 miles) long. Thousands of smaller rivers flow into it.
