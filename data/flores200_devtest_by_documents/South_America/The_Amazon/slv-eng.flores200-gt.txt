The Amazon River is the second longest and at the same time the largest river on Earth.It contains more than 8 times as much water as the second largest river.
Amazon, whose areas are up to six miles wide, is also the widest river on Earth.
As much as 20 percent of the water that flows into the oceans from the planet's rivers, comes from Amazon.
The main river Amazon is 6387 km long (3980 miles).Thousands of smaller rivers pour into it.
