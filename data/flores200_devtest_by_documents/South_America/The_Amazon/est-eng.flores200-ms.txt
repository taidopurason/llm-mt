The Amazon River is the second longest and largest river in the world. Eight times as much water flows in it as in the second largest river in the world.
The Amazon is also the widest river on earth, at times six miles wide.
20% of the water that flows from the world's rivers into the oceans comes from the Amazon.
The main river of the Amazon is 6387 km (3980 mi) long. Water from thousands of smaller rivers reaches it.
