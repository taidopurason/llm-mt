Why would an organization want to go through the time-consuming process of setting up a learning organization? One of the goals for implementing the concepts of a learning organization is innovation.
When all available resources are used effectively across the functional components of the organization, creativity and ingenuity can be manifested.
As a result, the process of a collaborative organization to overcome an obstacle can lead to new innovative processes to serve the customer's needs.
For an organization to be innovative, its leadership must create an innovative corporate culture, establish knowledge sharing and organizational learning.
Angel (2006) explains the Continuum approach as a method that organizations use to achieve higher levels of performance.
