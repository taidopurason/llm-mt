Why would the organization undergo a time -consuming process of establishing a learning organization?The transfer of concepts of organizational learning into practice is, among other things, an innovation.
When operating departments of the organization effectively use available resources, creativity and ingenuity can begin.
Thus, in the process of cooperation within the organization, a new innovative procedure can be created to meet the needs of the clients.
Before a particular organization can become innovative, the management must establish an innovative environment and promote common knowledge and organizational learning.
Angel (2006) explains the approach with continuity as a method used to help organizations in achieving a higher level of efficiency.
