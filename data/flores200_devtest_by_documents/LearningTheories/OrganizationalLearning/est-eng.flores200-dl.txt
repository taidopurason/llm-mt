Why would a company undertake the time-consuming task of setting up a learning organisation? One of the objectives of engaging business learning concepts is innovation.
When all the available resources are used effectively across the functional departments of an organisation, creativity and ingenuity will emerge.
As a result, a company working together to overcome an obstacle can lead to a new inventive process that meets a customer need.
Before an organisation can be innovative, management must create a culture of innovation, as well as shared knowledge and organisational learning.
Angel (2006) explains the continuum approach as a method used to help companies reach higher levels of performance.
