Why would an organization want to go through the time-consuming process of establishing a learning organization? One of the goals for implementing learning organization concepts is innovation.
If all available resources are used effectively across the functional components of the organization, creativity and ingenuity can be demonstrated.
As a result, the process of an organization working together to overcome an obstacle can lead to new innovative processes to serve customer needs.
For an organization to be innovative, its leadership must create an innovative corporate culture, implement knowledge sharing and organizational learning.
Angel (2006) explains the Continuum Approach as a method used by organizations to achieve higher levels of performance.
