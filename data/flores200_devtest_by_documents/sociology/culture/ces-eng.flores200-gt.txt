Subcultures combine similarly -minded individuals who feel neglected by social standards and allow them to develop an identity.
Subcultures may be different in terms of age, ethnic origin, class, location and/or gender of members.
The properties that distinguish the subculture may be linguistic, aesthetic, religious, political, sexual, geographical or a combination of factors.
Subculture members often show their membership through the distinctive and symbolic use of a style that includes dressing, behavior and slang.
