Subcultures combine like -minded people who are thought to be overlooked by social standards and allow them to develop their own identity.
Subcultures may vary in age, ethnicity, class, location and/or gender of its members.
The characteristics of which the subculture is recognizable can be linguistic, aesthetic, religious, political, sexual, geographical, or a combination of factors.
Members of the subculture often indicate their belonging to the recognizable and symbolic use of style, which includes fashion, behavior, and Argo.
