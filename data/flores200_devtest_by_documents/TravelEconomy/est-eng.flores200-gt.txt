The Oyapock River Bridge is a Vantsild.It extends across the Oyapock River, uniting the city of Saint-Georges de L'Oyapock in Brazil and the city of Saint-Georges de L'Oyapock.
Two towers reach 83 meters, the bridge is 378 meters long and has two 3.5 meters wide lanes.
The mileage height of the bridge is 15 meters.Construction was completed in August 2011, but was not open to traffic until March 2017.
It is planned that the bridge will be fully opened on September 2017, when Brazilian customs points are expected to be completed.
The official currency of the Falkland Islands is the Falkland Pound (FKP), which is value equal to one British nail (GBP).
Money can be exchanged in the only bank of the islands, located opposite the Fic West store in Stanley.
Generally, British nails are received everywhere on the islands, and in Stanley, credit cards and US dollars are often received.
Credit cards are unlikely to be accepted on the peripheral islands, although the currency of the United Kingdom and the United States may be accepted;Before advice with the owners to determine the appropriate payment method.
Changing the Falkland currency outside the islands is almost impossible, so exchange money before leaving the islands.
