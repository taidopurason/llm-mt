The Oyapock Bridge is a bridge with sloping grips.It extends above the Oyapock River and connects the city of Oiapoque in Brazil with the town of Saint Georges de l'oyapock in French Guyana.
The towers are 83 meters high, measuring 378 meters in length and have two 3.5 meters wide crossing.
The bridge stands 15 meters high.It was built in August 2011 and was opened only in March 2017.
The bridge is expected to be operated in September 2017, when Brazilian customs checkpoints are expected to be completed.
The official currency of the Falkland Islands is the Falkland Pun (FKP), and its value is determined equivalent to the British pound (GBP).
Cash can be replaced at the only bank on the islands located in Stanley opposite the FIC West store.
British pounds are accepted by virtually all islands, and at Stanley, they mostly receive credit cards and US dollars.
They are unlikely to receive credit cards on the outside of the lying islands, but they may accept British and US currency;For owners, know in advance which payment method is acceptable.
It is almost impossible to replace the Falkland currency outside the islands, so replace the cash before leaving the islands.
