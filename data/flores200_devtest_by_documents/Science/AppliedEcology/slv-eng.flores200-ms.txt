Natural tourism attracts people interested in visiting natural areas to enjoy the beauty of the landscape, including plants and wildlife.
Outreach activities include, but are not limited to, hunting, fishing, photography, bird watching and park visits, and the study of ecosystem information.
One example is watching, photographing and learning about orangutans in Borneo.
