People have known basic chemical elements such as gold, silver and copper since ancient times, as these elements are found in nature in their pure form and can be relatively easy to mine using simple tools.
The philosopher Aristotle believed that everything was composed of a mixture of one or more of the four elements.These elements were ground, water, air and fire.
It was more like four states of mass (in the same order): solid, liquid, gaseous and plasma, although he also theorized that it was turning into new substances that shape what we see.
