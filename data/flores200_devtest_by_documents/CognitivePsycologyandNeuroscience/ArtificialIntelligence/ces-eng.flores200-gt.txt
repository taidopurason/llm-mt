Although artificial intelligence has a strong connotation with science fiction, it forms a very important area of computer science dealing with behavior, learning and intelligence adaptation of machines.
Research on artificial intelligence involves creating machines that automate tasks requiring intelligent behavior.
Examples include checking, planning and schedule, the ability to respond to the diagnosis and questions of customers, handwriting, voice and face.
Such things have become separate disciplines that focus on providing solving real life problems.
The artificial intelligence system is now often used in areas of economy, medicine, engineering and military and is built in a number of home computers and video game software.
