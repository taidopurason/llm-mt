All the animals that originally arrived on the islands, joined, arrived, or were brought there by wind or sea streams.
Due to the long distance from the continent, the mammals were unable to overcome this distance and the giant turtle became the main grazing animal on the Galapagos.
Since the arrival of a man at the Galapagos, a number of mammals have been brought, including goats, horses, cows, rats, cats and dogs.
