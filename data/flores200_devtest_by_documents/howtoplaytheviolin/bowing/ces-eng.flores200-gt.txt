The “string up” mark looks like the letter V and the "loop down" looks like a staple or a square that is missing the underside.
Upstairs, it means that you start at the tip and push the string, it means that you start at the flip flops (where your hand holds the string) and pull the loop.
The upward movement usually causes a finer sound, while moving down is stronger and more penetrating.
You can write your own brands in a pencil with a pencil, but keep in mind that the printed brands have a reason from a musical point of view, so they should usually be followed.
