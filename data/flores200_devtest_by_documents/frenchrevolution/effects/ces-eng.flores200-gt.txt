There are many social and political consequences, such as using the metric system, transition from absolutism to Republicanism and nationalism or faith that the country belongs to people, not one rulers.
After the revolution, the professions were opened to all male applicants, making it possible to succeed in the most ambitious and successful ones.
The same applies to the army, because instead of military rank based on class belonging now depends on abilities.
The French Revolution also inspired many oppressed workers from other countries to try to start a revolution in their countries.
