European influence and colonialism began in the 15th century, when the Portuguese explorer Vasco da Gama discovered the sea route from Europe to India.
The modern city of Casablanca was founded by Berber fishermen in the 10th century BC. The Phoenicians, Romans and Maronites used it as a strategic port called Anfa.
The Portuguese destroyed it and rebuilt it under the name Casa Branca, but abandoned it after the earthquake of 1755.
The Moroccan sultan rebuilt the city Daru I-Badyna, and Spanish merchants who established trading centers there, gave it the name Casablanca
The capital of Moldova is Chişinău. The local language is Romanian, but Russian is also used a lot.
Moldova is a multi-ethnic republic that has suffered from ethnic conflict.
In 1994, this conflict led to the creation of the self-proclaimed Transnistrian Republic in eastern Moldova, which has its own government and currency, but has not been recognized by any UN member state.
Economic ties have been restored again between the two parts of Moldova, despite failures in political negotiations.
The main religion in Moldova is Orthodox Christianity.
