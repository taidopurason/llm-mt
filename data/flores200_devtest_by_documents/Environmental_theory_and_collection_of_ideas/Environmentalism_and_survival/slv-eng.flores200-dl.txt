We can make our lives more environmentally friendly, we can join the environmental movement, we can even become activists to reduce suffering to some extent in the future.
In many cases, this is similar to symptomatic treatment. However, if we do not want a temporary solution, we need to find the root of the problem and tackle it.
It is quite obvious that the world has changed enormously as a result of mankind's scientific and technological advances, but the problems have also increased as a result of overpopulation and man's wasteful lifestyle.
