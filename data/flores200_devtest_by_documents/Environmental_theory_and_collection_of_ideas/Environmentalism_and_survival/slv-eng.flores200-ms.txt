We can make our lives more environmentally friendly, we can join the environmental protection movement, we can even become activists to reduce suffering in the future to some extent.
This is in many cases similar to symptomatic treatment. However, if we do not just want a temporary solution, we must find the root of the problem and remedy it.
It is quite obvious that the world has changed tremendously as a result of the scientific and technological progress of mankind, and the problems have also been exacerbated by overpopulation and man's wasteful lifestyle.
