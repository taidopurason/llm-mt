To reduce future suffering to some extent, we can live more environmentally friendly, join the environmental movement, and even become activists.
In many cases, it is the same as symptomatic therapy. However, if we are not only looking for a temporary solution, we should find the cause of the problems and get rid of them.
It is clear that the world has changed a lot due to the scientific and technological progress of mankind, and problems have increased due to overpopulation and extravagant lifestyle of humanity.
