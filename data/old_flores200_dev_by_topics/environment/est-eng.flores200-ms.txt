Red currents are caused by a higher than usual concentration of Karenia brevis. It is a naturally occurring single-celled marine organism.
Natural factors, taken together, can create ideal conditions that make these algae multiply to a huge extent.
Algae forms a neurotoxin that paralyzes the nerves of both humans and fish.
Due to the high content of toxin in water bodies, fish often die.
People can be affected by inhalation of polluted water that has entered the air due to wind and waves.
