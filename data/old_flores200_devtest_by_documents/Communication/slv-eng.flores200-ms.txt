In remote places without a mobile signal, you may only be able to help yourself with a satellite phone.
A satellite phone usually cannot replace a mobile phone, as you need to be outdoors and have an unobstructed view of the satellite to make a call.
This service is widely used in shipping, including pleasure boats, as well as expeditions that need a remote data and voice service.
Your local telephone provider can give you more information about connecting to this service.
