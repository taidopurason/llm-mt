In remote places without a mobile signal, you may only be able to use a satellite phone.
A satellite phone is not usually a substitute for a mobile phone, as you need to be outdoors and have an unobstructed view of the satellite to make a call.
This service is often used by shipping services, including excursion vessels, as well as expeditions that need remote data and voice service.
Your local telephone service provider can provide you with more information on connecting to this service.
