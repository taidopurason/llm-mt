German pastries are pretty good and quite rich and varied in Bavaria, similar to those of our southern neighbour Austria.
Fruit orchards are commonplace, with apples baked in pastries throughout the year, and cherries and plums making their show in the summer.
In many German bakery products there are also almonds, hazelnuts and other nuts. Popular cakes often go well with a cup of strong coffee.
If you want some hard, opulent bakery products, try a pastry that, depending on the region, is called berliner, pfannkuchen or krapfen.
