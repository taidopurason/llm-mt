Why would an organisation undergo the time-consuming process of setting up a learning organisation? Putting organisational learning concepts into practice is one of the purposes of innovation.
When the working departments of an organisation make effective use of the available resources, creativity and ingenuity can begin.
In this way, a new innovative process can emerge in the process of working together within the organisation to overcome obstacles in order to meet the needs of customers.
Before an organisation can become innovative, management must create an innovative environment and foster shared knowledge and organisational learning.
Angel (2006) explains the continuity approach as a method used to help organisations achieve higher levels of performance.
