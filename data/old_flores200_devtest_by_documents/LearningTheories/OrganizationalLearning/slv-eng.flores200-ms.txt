Why would an organization undergo a time-consuming process of setting up a learning organization? The transfer of concepts of organisational learning into practice is intended, among other things, for innovation.
When working departments of an organization make effective use of available resources, creativity and ingenuity can begin.
Thus, in the process of cooperation within an organization in order to overcome obstacles, a new innovative process may arise that will meet the needs of customers.
Before an organisation can become innovative, management needs to establish an innovative environment and promote shared knowledge and organizational learning.
Angel (2006) explains the continuity approach as a method used to help organizations achieve higher levels of efficiency.
