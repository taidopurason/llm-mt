In Harappa and Mohenjo-Daro, there was a vesic cluster in almost every house connected to a complex sewerage system.
The remains of sewer systems have been found in Crete, Minos culture cities, and in Santorin, Greece.
The toilets were also available in ancient Egypt, Persia and China.In Roman civilization, toilets were sometimes part of the public hot houses with men and women confused.
