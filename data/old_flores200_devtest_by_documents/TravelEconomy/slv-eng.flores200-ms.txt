The bridge on the Oyapock River is a cable-stayed bridge. It extends over the Oyapock River and connects the city of Oiapoque in Brazil with the city of Saint Georges de l'Oyapock in French Guiana.
The towers are 83 meters high, 378 metres in length and have two 3.5 meters wide passageways.
The bridge stands 15 meters high. It was built in August 2011 and only opened for traffic in March 2017.
The bridge is expected to enter service in September 2017, when Brazil's customs checkpoints are also expected to be completed.
The official currency of the Falkland Islands is the Falkland Pound (FKP), and its value is determined equivalent to the value of the British pound sterling (GBP).
Cash can be exchanged at the only bank on the islands located in Stanley opposite the FIC West store.
British pounds are accepted on virtually all islands, and credit cards and US dollars are also mostly accepted on Stanley.
They are unlikely to accept credit cards on the Outlying Islands, but they may accept British and US currency; Check with the owners in advance which payment method is acceptable.
The currency of Falkland is almost impossible to exchange outside the islands, so exchange cash before you leave the islands.
