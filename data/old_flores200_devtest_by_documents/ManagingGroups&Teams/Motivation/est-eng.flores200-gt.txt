Theories of achievement need try to understand what people make and what they like.
According to these theories, people have certain needs and/or desires that are adopted during adulthood.
These theories deal with what makes certain people want the things they do, and what environmental factors make these people do or not to do certain things.
The two most successful need -based theory is the theory of Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs and the theory of Herzberg's two factors.
