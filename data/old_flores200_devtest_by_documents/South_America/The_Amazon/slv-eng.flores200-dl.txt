The Amazon River is the second longest and largest river on Earth. It contains more than 8 times as much water as the second largest river.
With stretches up to six miles wide, the Amazon is also the widest river on Earth.
As much as 20% of the water that flows from the planet's rivers into the oceans comes from the Amazon.
The main river, the Amazon, is 3,980 miles (6,387 km) long, and is fed by thousands of smaller rivers.
