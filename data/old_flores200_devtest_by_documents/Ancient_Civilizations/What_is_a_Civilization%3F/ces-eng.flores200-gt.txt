Civilization is a certain culture, a common large group of people who live and work in mutual cooperation, ie together they form society.
The word civilization comes from the Latin "civilis", which means "civil" and it is related to the Latin "civis", which means "citizen" and "civitas", which means a city or city state, and at the same time defines in some way the size of society.
The predecessors of the nations were city states.The culture of civilization is characterized by the transmission of knowledge for several generations, the presence of long -lasting cultural tracks and adequate spread.
Minor cultures often disappear without leaving relevant historical evidence and are not recognized as proper civilizations.
