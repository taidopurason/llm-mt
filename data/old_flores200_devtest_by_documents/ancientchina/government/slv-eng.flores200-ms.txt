Ancient China depicted different periods of time in a special way; Every stage of China, or every family in power, was a recognizable dynasty.
There was also an unstable period between individual dynasties when the provinces were divided. Among these periods, the most famous period is the three kingdoms between the Han and Jin dynasties, which lasted 60 years.
During these periods, fierce struggles took place between many nobles who were the battle for the throne.
The Three Kingdoms period was one of the bloodiest periods in ancient China's history, killing thousands of people fighting for the tallest chair in the Grand Palace of the city of Shyan.
