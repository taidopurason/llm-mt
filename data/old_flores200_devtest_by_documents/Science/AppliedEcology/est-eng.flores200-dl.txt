Nature tourism attracts people who are interested in visiting natural places to enjoy the views and also wild plants and animals.
Examples of on-the-ground activities include hunting, fishing, photography, birdwatching and visiting parks and learning about the ecosystem.
An example would be visiting, photographing and learning about the Boreno orangutans.

In this dynamic transport system, everyone is in some way connected to and supported by a private vehicle-based transport system.
