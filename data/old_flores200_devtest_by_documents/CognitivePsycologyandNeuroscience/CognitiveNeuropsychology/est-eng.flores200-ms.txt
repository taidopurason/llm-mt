Neurobiological data present physical evidence for a theoretical approach to the study of cognition. Thus, it delineates the field of research and makes it more accurate.
The link between brain pathology and behavior supports research by scientists.
It has long been known that different types of brain injuries, traumas, injuries and tumors affect behavior and cause alterations in some mental functions.
The rise of new technologies allows us to see and study brain structures and processes that have never been seen before.
It gave us a lot of information and material to create simulation models that help us better understand the processes taking place in our brains.
