Although artificial intelligence has a strong connotation with science fiction, it forms a very important area of computer science dealing with the behavior, learning, and intelligence adaptation of machines.
Artificial intelligence research involves creating machines that automate tasks that require intelligent action.
Examples include checking, scheduling, and scheduling, the ability to respond to diagnoses and customer questions, handwriting, voice, and face recognition.
Such things have become separate disciplines that focus on providing solutions to real life problems.
The artificial intelligence system is now widely used in the fields of economics, medicine, engineering and military, and is built into a variety of home computer applications and video game software.
