Aerosmith cancelled the remaining concerts of their tour.
The rock band should be touring the United States and Canada until September 16.
The tour was cancelled after lead singer Steven Tyler was injured on August 5 during a performance due to a fall from the stage.
The program started at 20:30 local time (15:00 UTC).
Famous singers from all over the country paid tribute to Shri Shyama's feet with bayans or liturgical songs.
The evening was opened by singer Sanju Sharma, followed by Jai Shankar Choudhary. Lakkha Singh also introduced bajan chhappan bhog. He was accompanied by singer Raju Khandelwal.
The initiative in singing the bayans was then taken by Lakkha Singh.
Grandma Shyam served 108 plates of chappan bhoga (in Hinduism, this means 56 different dishes, such as sweets, fruits, nuts, prepared dishes, etc., offered to the deity).
Lakkha Singh also introduced bajan chhappan bhog. He was accompanied by singer Raju Khandelwal.
