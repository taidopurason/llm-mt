Aerosmith have cancelled the remaining shows of their tour.
The rock band was scheduled to perform a tour of the United States and Canada through Sept. 16.
The tour was cancelled after frontman Steven Tyler was injured in a fall from the stage during a performance on August 5.
The program started at 20:30 local time (15:00 UTC).
Famous singers from across the country presented bhajans or songs of devotion at the feet of Shri Shyam.
Singer Sanju Sharma opened the evening followed by Jai Shankar Choudhary. Lakkha Singh also sang a chhappan bhog bhajan. He was accompanied by singer Raju Khandelwal.
Thereafter, Lakkha Singh took over the singing of the bhajans.
Baba Shyam received 108 plates of Chhappan Bhog (in Hinduism 56 different types of food like sweets, fruits, nuts, dishes etc offered to the deity).
Then Lakkha Singh also presented the chhappan bhog bhajan. He was accompanied by singer Raju Khandelwal.
