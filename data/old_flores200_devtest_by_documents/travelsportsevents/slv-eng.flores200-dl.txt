The Paralympic Games will take place between 24 August and 5 September 2021. Some events will be held in other locations across Japan.
Tokyo will be the only Asian city to host the Summer Olympics twice, the first being in 1964.
If you have booked flights and accommodation for 2020 before the postponement, you may be in a difficult situation.
Cancellation policies vary, but most of the late March coronavirus cancellation policies do not apply to July 2020, when the Olympics are due to take place.
Most tickets are expected to cost between 2 500 and 130 000 yen, while normal tickets will cost around 7 000 yen.
