The Paralympics will take place from 24 August to 5 September 2021. Some events will be arranged at different places in Japan.
Tokyo will be the only Asian city that hosted two Summer Olympics because it was a host already in 1964.
If you have booked tickets and accommodation for 2020 before the delay announcement, this situation may be difficult for you.
The principles of interference of events may vary, but at the end of March, most of the principles of interference of coronavir -based events did not apply to July 2020, which were scheduled for the Olympic Games.
Tickets for most events are expected to cost between 2,500 and 130,000 Japanese yen, with regular tickets to be 7,000 Jen.
