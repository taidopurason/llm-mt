To reduce future suffering to some extent, we can live more environmentally friendly lives, join the environmental movement and even become activists.
In many cases, this is the same as symptomatic treatment. However, if we are not only after a temporary solution, we should find the cause of the problems and get rid of them.
It is obvious that the world has changed a lot due to the scientific and technological progress of mankind and the problems have increased due to overpopulation and the extravagant lifestyle of mankind.
