To go some way towards reducing future suffering, we can start living greener, join the environmental movement and even become activists.
In many cases, this is just like treating the symptoms. But if we don't want just a temporary solution, we need to find the root cause of the problem and make it harmless.
It is quite clear that the world has changed a great deal as a result of scientific and technological advances made by mankind, and that the problems have become greater as a result of overpopulation and mankind's wasteful lifestyle.
