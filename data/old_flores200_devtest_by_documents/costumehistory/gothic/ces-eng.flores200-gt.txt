The Gothic style reached its peak between 10-11.and the 14th century.
In the beginning, the dress was significantly influenced by Byzantine culture.
Due to slow communication, however, Western styles lagged by 25 to 30 years.
At the end of the Middle Ages Western Europe began to develop its own style.One of the greatest progress of this time was that, as a result of crusades, people began to use buttons to fasten clothing.
