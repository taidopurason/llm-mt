On Monday, Sara Danius, the permanent secretary of the Nobel Committee for Literature of the Swedish Academy, publicly announced during the radio broadcast on Sveriges that the committee, which could not directly inform Bob Dylan that he had received the Nobel Prize in Literature 2016, abandoned his effortswould find it.
Danius said, "We do nothing right now. I called his closest coworker and sent him several emails, and I received very friendly answers. For now, this is definitely enough."
