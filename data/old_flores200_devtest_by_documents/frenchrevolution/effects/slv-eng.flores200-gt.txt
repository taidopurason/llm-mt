There are many social and political effects, such as the use of a metric system, the transition from absolutism to Republicanism, nationalism and the belief that the state belongs to the people, not a single ruler.
In addition, after the revolution, the professions were open to all male seekers, allowing them to succeed the most ambitious and successful.
This also applies to the army, as military ranks are now based on caliber instead of on the class.
The French Revolution also inspired many other oppressed members of the working class from other countries to start their revolutions.
