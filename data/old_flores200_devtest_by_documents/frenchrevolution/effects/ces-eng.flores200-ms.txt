There are many social and political consequences, such as the use of the metric system, the transition from absolutism to republicanism and nationalism, or the belief that the land belongs to the people, not to one ruler.
After the revolution, the professions were open to all male applicants, allowing the most ambitious and successful to succeed.
The same is true of the military, as instead of class-based military ranks, it is now skill that matters.
The French Revolution also inspired many oppressed workers from other countries to try to start a revolution in their countries.
