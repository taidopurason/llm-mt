A day in traffic: three vehicles were involved in an accident in the center of Tallinn
Three cars were involved in an accident in the center of Tallinn yesterday morning, one of which was parked on the side of the street.
At 9:53 a.m., a traffic accident occurred at the intersection of Pronksi tn and Gonsiori tn in Tallinn, where, according to preliminary data, a 38-year-old man was driving an Audi R8 and collided with an Audi Q7 driven by a 44-year-old woman.
As a result of the collision, the Audi R8 was thrown into the Ford Tourneo.
The driver of the Audi Q7 was taken to Ida-Tallinn Central Hospital.
The exact circumstances of the accident are being clarified.
At 3:20 p.m., a 48-year-old woman drove a Mercedes-Benz passenger car into a 75-year-old woman crossing the road at Jüri Street 46 in Võru.
The driver took the pedestrian to Võru Hospital for a check-up.
During the previous 24 hours, one driver of a motor vehicle with signs of drug and one alcohol consumption was caught.
A 2004 gray Volvo V40 with license plate number 789BSN was stolen from the administrative area of Southern Prefecture.
