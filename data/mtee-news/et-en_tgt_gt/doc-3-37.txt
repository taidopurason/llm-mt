Very expensive clothes racks will be provided for the new gym in Kohtla-Järve with state money
The list of property costs of the Kohtla-Järve Wiru sports center, opened this year, particularly stands out for the cost of the racks needed in the gym's locker room.
The manager of the sports center justifies the extremely expensive racks with the fact that they want to be the most modern.
