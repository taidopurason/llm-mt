The family doctor warns: The flu is on the move!
It is a serious illness, it is not just a cold and cough.
How to protect yourself?
Much is said about coronavirus, its prevention and treatment.
Next to this, one tends to forget that there are many different respiratory viruses on the move in the autumn-winter season, and the flu has already reached Estonia, so it is high time to protect itself from the virus, says Triinu-Mari Ots, Head of the Laagri Family Practice Center and family doctor, advice to recognize the fluAnd for prevention.
A typical Estonian autumn-winter cough-deaf-throat season has begun, with acute viral infections of the upper respiratory tract caused by various viruses on the move.
“The most common causes are rhinovirus, RS virus, adenovirus and parainfluenza.
The general name of these diseases is acute respiratory viral infections and are treated with a specific symptom, be it cough, sore throat, runny nose or headache, ”says Triinu-Mari Ots.
The exception is the influenza virus, which is very specific and can be determined by symptoms in the early days of the disease.
"In this case, analyzes are also taken to provide a laboratory confirmation of the presence of influenza virus," says the family doctor.
This is important for the prescribing and restraining or restricted disease in the workplace, in the gym or school, for example.
