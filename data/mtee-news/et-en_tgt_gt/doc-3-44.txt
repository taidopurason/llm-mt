A great example: Anett Kontaveit does not sit idly by on vacation and helps those in need
After the tiring tournament in Mexico, Estonia took some time off from tennis and is resting in its homeland.
But during this he helps those who need help.
Anett Kontaveit does not just rest with her hands folded, but makes practical use of this time.
She posted pictures on Instagram of herself as a "plant mom" growing plants that provide greenery at home.
And also engages in charity work, increasing awareness of the activities of Varjupaikade MTÜ.
Anett posted pictures of the dogs on social media, saying that she will help them again this year.
At Monday's press conference, Anett Kontaveit said that she has thought about winning the Grand Slam tournament.
“I've been thinking about it for a few days.
Of course, my dream is to win a Grand Slam, but the most important thing is that the goals are based on development.
I want to improve my game and certain elements of tennis, as well as my physique.
If this development process is positive, better results will come," he said via Delfi Sport.
Kontaveit thanked the fans who stayed up late at night for her games in Mexico in recent weeks: “It's been very heartwarming how many people have supported me.
I have received so much positive feedback and I am very grateful to everyone who cares about what I do.
I also thank the supporters and sponsors, thanks to whom I have reached here."
After a short vacation, Kontaveit will start preparing for the new season in Tallinn, and as the Australian tournaments approach, she will fly to a warmer place to acclimatize.
