A moderate rise on the Baltic stock exchange today
The Tallinn Stock Exchange has risen by 0.19% compared to November 24.
The stock price of Bercman Technologies has made a perceptible fall.
In the Baltic First North list, the Bercman Technologies share has fallen from EUR 9 to EUR 8.6 in the last day, which means a 4.44% decrease.
Compared to the fall from 11.4 euros to 9 euros (-21.05%) on November 24, Bercman's share price is currently stabilizing.
The share of ELMO Rent has also gone down after the good news at the beginning of the week.
As of today, the share has fallen to 5.03 euros (-2.9%).
On the other hand, Baltic First North's K2 LT is increasing to 31.2 euros (2.63%), and MADARA Cosmetics also made a slight increase of 0.71% to 28.2 euros.
In the Baltic main list, today's biggest gainers are Panevezio statybos tresta to 0.7 euro (5.74%) and SAF Tehnika to 12.5 euro (3.73%).
The Baltic additional list had the biggest increase and was in Utenos Trikotaza to 0.815 euros (3.82%).
Today's biggest fallers in the main Baltic list were Griego to EUR 0.95 (-1.45%) and Ekspress Grupp to EUR 1.53 (-1.29%).
In the additional list, the value of the VEF share fell noticeably to 5.1 euros (-8.93%).
