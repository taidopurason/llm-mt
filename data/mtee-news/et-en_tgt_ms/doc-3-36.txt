Moderate rise on the Baltic stock exchange today
The Tallinn stock exchange has risen by 0.19% compared to 24 November.
The stock price of Bercman Technologies has undergone a noticeable fall.
In the Baltic First North list, Bercman Technologies' shares have fallen from 9 euros to 8.6 euros in the past day, which means a decrease of 4.44%.
Compared to the fall on November 24 from 11.4 euros to 9 euros (-21.05%), Bercman's share price is currently stabilizing.
ELMO Rent's stock has also gone into decline after the good news of the beginning of the week.
As of today, the share has fallen to 5.03 euros (-2.9%).
On the other hand, Baltic First North is rising from K2 TO 31.2 euros (2.63%) and MADARA Cosmetics also made a slight increase of 0.71% to 28.2 euros.
The biggest risers on the Baltic Main List today are Panevezio statybos trestas 0.7 euros (5.74%) and SAF Tehnika 12.5 euros (3.73%).
The biggest riser on the Baltic additional list was Utenos Trikotazas to 0.815 euros (3.82%).
In the Baltic Main List, the biggest fallouts today were Griego to 0.95 euros (-1.45%) and Ekspress Grupp to 1.53 euros (-1.29%).
In the secondary list, the value of VEF's share dropped significantly to 5.1 euros (-8.93%).
