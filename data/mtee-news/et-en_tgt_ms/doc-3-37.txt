In the new gym in Kohtla-Järve, ultra-expensive clothes racks are stocked with state money
The list of property costs of the Kohtla-Järve Wiru Sports Centre, which opened this year, highlights the cost of racks needed in the gym's dressing room.
The head of the fitness center justifies the extremely expensive racks by the fact that they want to be the most modern.
