A day in traffic: Three vehicles were involved in an accident in the center of Tallinn
In the center of Tallinn, three cars were involved in an accident yesterday morning, one of which was standing on the side of the street.
At 9:53 a.m., a traffic accident occurred at the intersection of Pronksi St. and Gonsiori St. in Tallinn, where, according to preliminary data, a 38-year-old man was driving an Audi R8 and collided with an Audi Q7 car driven by a 44-year-old woman.
As a result of the collision, a Ford Tourneo, a passenger car that was standing against the Audi R8, was thrown in.
The driver of the Audi Q7 was delivered to the East Tallinn Central Hospital.
The exact circumstances of the accident are being clarified.
At 3.20 p.m., a 48-year-old woman was driving a Mercedes-Benz car near the house at 46 Jüri St. in Võru city to a 75-year-old woman who had crossed the road.
The driver transported the pedestrian to Võru Hospital for an inspection.
One driver of a motor vehicle with signs of drug and one alcohol use was apprehended the previous day.
A 2004 grey Volvo V40 with the number 789BSN was stolen from the southern prefecture's administrative area.
