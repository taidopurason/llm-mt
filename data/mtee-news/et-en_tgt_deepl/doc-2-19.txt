ANU SÄÄRITS ON HONEST LIVING: Objectivity and the courage to stand up straight and not underestimate yourself or others are essential.
Anu's childhood home has taught her that no matter what, you have to manage.
"I come from a big family.
In our family, the mother looked after five children practically on her own, we could not make any demands.
I had to grow up fast myself in order to somehow realise my interests."
Curiosity has indeed been the driving force behind Anu Sääritsa (49), a sports news and broadcast personality, since childhood.
"Quite early on I saw that there was something interesting going on in Tallinn!
I decided to drive all the way from Tartu, check it out, and only when I got back to tell my family that I had been there," she laughs, recalling her determination at the time, which led her to repeatedly visit further afield.
