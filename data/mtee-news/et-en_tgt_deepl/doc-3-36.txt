Moderate rise on the Baltic stock exchange today
The Tallinn stock exchange is up 0.19% since 24 November.
The share price of Bercman Technologies has undergone a significant fall.
On the Baltic First North list, Bercman Technologies shares have fallen from €9 to €8.6 in the last day, down 4.44%.
Compared to the fall from €11.4 to €9 (-21.05%) on 24 November, Bercman's share price is now stabilising.
ELMO Rent's share price has also gone down after the good news at the beginning of the week.
As of today, the share has fallen to €5.03 (-2.9%).
On the other hand, Baltic First North has K2 LT rising to €31.2 (2.63%) and MADARA Cosmetics also made a slight gain of 0.71% to €28.2.
In the Baltic main list, the biggest risers today are Panevezio statybos trestas at EUR 0.7 (5.74%) and SAF Tehnika at EUR 12.5 (3.73%).
Utenos Trikotazas was the biggest gainer in the Baltic supplement at €0.815 (3.82%).
In the Baltic main list, today's biggest fallers were Griego at EUR 0.95 (-1.45%) and Ekspress Grupp at EUR 1.53 (-1.29%).
In the supplementary listing, the value of VEF shares fell sharply to €5.1 (-8.93%).
Žemaitijos pienas shares also fell to €1.7 (-5.03%).
