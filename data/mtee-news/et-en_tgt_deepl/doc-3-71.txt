A day in traffic: three vehicles involved in an accident in central Tallinn
Three cars were involved in an accident in central Tallinn yesterday morning, one of which was parked on the side of the road.
At 9.53 a.m., a traffic accident occurred at the intersection of Pronksi Street and Gonsiori Street in Tallinn, where, according to preliminary information, a 38-year-old man driving an Audi R8 collided with an Audi Q7 driven by a 44-year-old woman.
The collision caused the Audi R8 to slam into a stationary Ford Tourneo.
The driver of the Audi Q7 was taken to the East Tallinn Central Hospital.
The exact circumstances of the accident are still being clarified.
At 15.20, a 48-year-old woman driving a Mercedes-Benz hit a 75-year-old woman crossing the road near a house at Jüri Street 46 in Võru.
The driver of the vehicle took the pedestrian to Võru Hospital for a check-up.
During the previous day, one drug and one alcohol-impaired driver were caught.
A grey 2004 Volvo V40 with the registration number 789BSN was stolen from the Southern Prefecture.
