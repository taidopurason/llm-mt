The red currents are caused by higher than normal concentrations of Karenia brevis. It is a naturally occurring single-celled marine organism.
Natural factors can combine to create the ideal conditions for these algae to proliferate at enormous rates.
Algae form a neurotoxin that paralyses the nerves of both humans and fish.
High levels of toxins in water bodies often kill fish.
Humans can be affected by inhaling contaminated water released into the air by wind and waves.
