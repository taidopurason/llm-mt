Red currents are caused by a higher than normal concentration of Karenia brevis.It is a naturally occurring unicellular marine organism.
Natural factors can combine to create the ideal conditions that cause these algae to multiply to an enormous extent.
Algae forms a neurotoxin that paralyzes the nerves of both humans and fish.
Due to the high content of the toxin in water bodies, fish often die.
Humans can be affected by inhaling contaminated water released into the air by wind and waves.
